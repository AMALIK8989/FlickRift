Atomic Blonde

"Atomic Blonde" is a thrilling action film set against the backdrop of the Cold War era. Starring Charlize Theron as MI6 agent Lorraine Broughton, the story unfolds in Berlin just before the fall of the Berlin Wall. Broughton is tasked with retrieving a valuable dossier and navigating a city rife with espionage, betrayal, and deadly secrets.
