Tell It To The Bees: 2017

Description

"Tell It to the Bees" is a poignant romantic drama set in 1950s rural Scotland, adapted from Fiona Shaw's novel.

The story follows Dr. Jean Markham, played by Anna Paquin, who returns to her hometown to take over her late father's medical practice. Her arrival sparks intrigue and controversy when she befriends Lydia, a young mother played by Holliday Grainger, and they develop a deep emotional connection that challenges societal norms and prejudices of the time. Against the backdrop of a small community and the mystical allure of beekeeping, "Tell It to the Bees" explores themes of love, acceptance, and the courage to defy societal expectations.
