Tekken: 2010

Description

Tekken (2010) is a pulse-pounding martial arts action film inspired by the iconic video game series. Set in a dystopian future ruled by powerful mega-corporations, the story follows Jin Kazama, a rebellious street fighter who enters the brutal Iron Fist Tournament to avenge his mother’s death. As Jin rises through the ranks, he faces elite combatants from across the globe—each with their own fighting style, deadly skills, and hidden motives.

With intense hand-to-hand combat, gritty visuals, and a dark sci-fi setting, Tekken delivers raw energy and fierce showdowns that capture the essence of the beloved franchise. Whether you're a longtime fan of the game or just in the mood for non-stop martial arts action, this film will leave you breathless.

Now streaming on FlickRift—watch Jin, Kazuya, Heihachi, and Nina battle for survival in a world where every punch writes history.
