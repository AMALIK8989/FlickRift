Kiss Me (Kyss Mig) - 2011

Description

"Kiss Me (Kyss Mig)" is a beautiful Swedish romantic drama about self-discovery, love, and the complexities of relationships. The film follows Mia and Frida, two women who meet and fall in love, challenging societal norms and their own expectations.
