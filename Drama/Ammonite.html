Ammonite : (2020)

Description

Set in 1840s England, "Ammonite" follows the story of fossil hunter Mary Anning (Kate Winslet) and her unexpected relationship with Charlotte Murchison (Saoirse Ronan). This historical drama beautifully explores themes of love, loneliness, and resilience.
