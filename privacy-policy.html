Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Flick Rift—your no-login, no-account, no-drama streaming zone. This Privacy Policy lays down the what, how, and why (or rather, why not) behind our data practices. We're not here to harvest your life story. We're just here to stream and chill—with zero need for your phone number, your cousin’s birthday, or your blood type.

2. What We Don’t Collect

Let’s get this straight: We don’t collect personal data. No usernames, no passwords, no registrations. You don’t have to give us your name, email, or your secret love for soap operas. There’s literally nowhere to input any of that—and that’s by design.

3. Cookies and Passive Data

Now for the part we *do* care about: cookies. Yep, we use them. They’re not chocolate chip, but they help us know which parts of Flick Rift you vibe with the most. They help with things like:

  • Page load optimization
  • User behavior tracking (non-personal)
  • Basic analytics (popular content, session length)
  • Ad placement and frequency capping

You can kill cookies in your browser settings. But fair warning—parts of the site might act weird or lag like a bad WiFi signal.

4. Do Not Track (DNT)

Your browser might shout “Do Not Track.” We respect that in spirit—but honestly, not all our tools recognize the signal. If you really don’t want tracking, block cookies directly in your browser. That’s the only surefire way.

5. Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted services to keep Flick Rift running smooth, including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Cloudflare (performance + DDoS protection)
  • Advertising partners (limited)

These services may drop their own cookies or pixels—again, not to spy, but to improve performance and deliver relevant ads. Their policies apply to their stuff. We don’t control it, but we don’t send them your personal data either. Why? Because we don’t have any.

6. Advertisements

You might see ads on Flick Rift. Advertisers may use cookies to avoid showing you the same boring ad 5 times in a row. We don’t allow explicit data sharing with advertisers. They target you based on your device/browser behavior—not your identity.

7. Security Measures

No user data means no risk of data breaches. But we still encrypt our site, secure our backend, and monitor for malicious activity. Your peace of mind is built into our code.

8. Children’s Privacy

Flick Rift is not intended for children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect info from kids. Parents, if your kid is streaming here—maybe have a talk. Or two.

9. Legal Stuff

By using Flick Rift, you agree to this policy. If any legal beef comes up, it’ll be handled under the laws of Pakistan, no matter where you’re streaming from. Any disputes will be handled in Karachi courts. Yes, we said that out loud.

10. Changes to This Policy

Policies evolve, especially on the internet. If we make changes, we’ll post them here. No drama, no backdoor updates. It’s your right to stay informed.
