Girl Play : 2004

Description

Robin (Robin Greenspan) and Lacie (Lacie Harmon) are cast as the lead lovers in a play. But the actresses have opposing personalities, and Robin is in a long-term relationship while Lacie enjoys her free-spirited single life. However, during rehearsals, an erotic spark ignites, leaving them to wonder if their attraction is a product of their art or the beginning of true love. Their mutual passion is so strong, they consider leaving their old lives and loves for a new romance together.
